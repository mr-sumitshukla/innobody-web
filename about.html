About Our Company

Welcome To Our INNOBODY-SYSTEMS

Innobody Systems Private Limited is the leading innovator for healthcare at home and workplaces, incorporated as a company in 2018, when one of our founding directors, Dr. Ruchi Sood (PT) got a chance to learn entrepreneurship at IIM, Bangalore, one of the finest business institutes in the world, with the focus on building innovative products & services in Physiotherapy And Home Health industry.

Innobody offers solutions that help improve care for all ages with the help of care professionals and Technology. Trusted by more than 2000 patients and many hospitals of Bhopal, Medanta Hospital-Indore, Lal Patho labs, URC Construction Pvt. Ltd. (Bhopal Metro), and The Nest (Aakriti Group).
